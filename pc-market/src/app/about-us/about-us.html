Our platform offers a wide range of features including:

    We do not hide good or bad reviews, simple as that. Reviews are posted by verified customers to promote transparency and trust.

    PC Market offers secure payment options for both buyers and sellers. Besides 30-day buyer protection, sellers are verified to ensure a safe trading environment.

    Refunds or complaints? We are here to help you with everything you need!

WANT TO BE A SELLER?

Ready to sell your PC or laptop? Our platform makes it easy to reach a wide audience. To ensure a safe and trustworthy marketplace, we require all sellers to meet a few simple criteria:

  • Be a registered PC Market user.
  • Be at least 18 years of age. Users aged 16-17 may register with parental consent.
  • Account must be in good standing.
  • Provide a valid ID for identity verification.
  • Have a valid bank account for secure payment processing.
  • Agree to our Seller's Code of Conduct.

If you meet these requirements, you're ready to get started!
