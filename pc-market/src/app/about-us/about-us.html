Seller's Code of Conduct

By becoming a seller on PC Market, you agree to adhere to our Seller's Code of Conduct, which includes:

  • Honesty and Transparency: Provide accurate product details including condition, specs, and any defects.
  • Customer Service: Respond promptly to buyer inquiries and resolve issues professionally.
  • Compliance: Follow all laws and regulations related to online selling.
  • Respect: Treat buyers with professionalism and courtesy.
  • No Fraudulent Activity: Do not engage in any misrepresentation, fake reviews, or scams.
  • Sensitive Information: Never share personal or sensitive data about yourself or others.

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these guidelines to maintain a safe and trustworthy marketplace. Any fraudulent activity will be reported to the relevant authorities.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We understand you might have some questions about our platform. For that, we’ve compiled a list of the most frequently asked questions to help you navigate PC Market with ease.

How do I create an account?

Click on the “Sign Up” button at the top right of the homepage and fill in the required information. Once your email is verified, you can start exploring the platform.

How does your 30-day buyer protection work?

Our 30-day buyer protection ensures you can shop with confidence. If your item doesn’t arrive or isn’t as described, file a claim within 30 days for a refund or replacement.

Does PC Market add fees for buyers or sellers?

PC Market is committed to transparency.
Buyers: no hidden fees.
Sellers: a small commission of either 3% or $100, whichever is greater.

Do you charge for shipping?

PC Market does not charge for shipping. Shipping costs are set by the seller and depend on location and shipping method.
