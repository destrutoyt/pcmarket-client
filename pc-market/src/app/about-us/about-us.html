Seller's Code of Conduct

By becoming a seller on PC Market, you agree to adhere to our Seller's Code of Conduct, which includes:

  • Honesty and Transparency: Provide accurate information about product including condition, specifications, and any defects.
  • Customer Service: Respond promptly to buyer inquiries and resolve issues professionally.
  • Compliance: Do not post illegal content. Follow all applicable laws and regulations related to selling online.
  • Respect: Treat buyers with respect and professionalism at all times.
  • No Fraudulent Activity: Do not engage in any form of fraud, including fake reviews or misrepresentation of products.
  • Sensitive information: Do not share personal or sensitive information about yourself or others.

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these guidelines to maintain a safe and trustworthy marketplace for all users.
Be aware that fraudulent activity (scams, phishing, etc.) will be reported to the relevant authorities.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We understand you might have some questions about our platform. For that, we have compiled a list of the most frequently asked questions to help you navigate PC Market with ease.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of the homepage and fill in the required information. Once you verified your email, you can start exploring the platform.

How does your 30-day buyer protection work?

Our 30-day buyer protection ensures that you can shop with confidence. If you encounter any issues with your purchase, such as not receiving the item or receiving a significantly different product, you can file a claim within 30 days of delivery.

Our support team will investigate the issue and work towards a resolution, which may include a refund or replacement.

Does PC Market add fees for buyers or sellers?

PC Market is committed to transparency.

For buyers, there are no hidden fees.
For sellers, we charge a small commission fee on each sale. Either 3% or $100, whichever is greater.

Do you charge for shipping?

PC Market does not charge for shipping. Shipping costs are determined by the seller and depend on the buyer's location and chosen shipping method.
